Benefits of Going Electric

A Fun and Quiet drive

Electric motors deliver 100% torque every time you hit the accelerator. The engines are near-silent and have no vibration. Electric motors are a time-tested technology with few moving parts. That means an EV's powertrain generally requires little maintenance, is highly efficient, and doesn't require an oil change. Even your brake pads get a break because electric cars use the motor to slow down, reducing wear
